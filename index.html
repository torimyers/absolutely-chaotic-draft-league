ğŸ¯ Key Concept: PPR vs Standard Scoring

The scoring system dramatically affects player values. In PPR (Points Per Reception), pass-catching players get bonus points for every catch, making them more valuable than in Standard scoring.

Example Impact:
Standard Scoring:
RBs more valuable
Big-play WRs preferred
TEs have less value
PPR Scoring:
Pass-catching RBs valuable
Slot WRs get boost
Elite TEs become crucial
ğŸ’¡ Draft Strategy: In PPR, target players with high reception totals earlier. A WR with 100 catches adds 100 points compared to Standard scoring!
